When does the application open? When does it close?

It opens for submission on August 1, 2016 at 6:00 PM EDT and closes on September 16, 2016 at 11:59 PM EDT.


Who can apply?

Applicants must meet the following criteria to be eligible for the ANTIGUA10X Challenge:


  • Applicants must be working in the region of Central America.
  • Applicants must be 18 years of age or older at the time of entry.
  • Applications must be submitted in English or Spanish.
  • Applicants can be citizens of any nationality.
  • Top ten winners are required to travel to Antigua, Guatemala for the finals October 15th and top 3 winners must be able to attend ANTIGUA10X Festival November 18, 19, 2016.
  • Winners agree to participate in 6 month mentorship and provide updates on their organization to ANTIGUA10X.

When will I find out if I am a finalist?

You will be notified of your application status by October 1, 2016.


When and where is ANTIGUA10X?

The finals are on October 15th and the Festival on November 18, 19th at the Antigua International School, Antigua, Guatemala


I know if I can’t attend the ANTIGUA10X in October 15th or November 18, 19th. Can I still apply?

Unfortunately, no. Festival attendance is mandatory for all winners. If I am a winner, how will I get to the Antigua10X Festival? The top three winners must provide their own transportation, but hotel accomodations will be provided for 2 adults in the same room.


Can I submit any further supporting documents or media?

Yes, you can submit up to 2 additional supporting materials, which can include documents (no more than 5 pages) and 3 min video uploaded to Youtube to support your application.
