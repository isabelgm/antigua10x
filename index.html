Antigua 10x Festival

Exchange leapfrogging ideas and practices to improve communities in a sustainable way.

Event

Join us for two days of amazing conferences, great discussions, interactive workshops, and fun.

Antigua10x is a two day event held in Antigua, Guatemala that brings together entrepreneurs, thinkers and changemakers from around the world to share insights and connect. Come away with a sense of how you can create 10x results in your community.

November 18-19, 2016

Think

The first part of the conference consists of a series of lighting talk where our rockstar speakers introduce ideas they've been working on. Think critically about new ideas.

Discuss

Go deeper into the ideas presented by the speakers through a series of panel discussions. The conversation is free-flowing among speakers and participants and the moderator.

Do

Finally, put into practice what you've learned through hands on workshops lead by our speakers. 3 companies will be selected from our Contest to be the case study for the workshops.
