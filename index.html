Antigua 10x Festival

November 18-19, 2016

Exchange leapfrogging ideas and practices to improve communities in a sustainable way.

November 18-19, 2016

Join us for two days of amazing conferences, great discussions, interactive workshops, and fun.

Antigua10x is a two day event held in Antigua, Guatemala that brings together entrepreneurs, thinkers and changemakers from around the world to share insights and connect. Come away with a sense of how you can create 10x results in your community.

Get Tickets Now

Think

The first part of the conference consists of a series of lighting talks given by innovators in tech, education and business. They will be sharing ideas that they've implemented to maximize results in their areas.

Discuss

Go deeper into the ideas presented by the speakers through a series of panel discussions. Discover the secrets of implementing these strategies. The conversation is free-flowing among speakers, participants and the moderator.

Do

Finally, put into practice what you've learned through hands on workshops lead by our speakers. 3 companies will be selected from our Central American Challenge to be the case study for the workshops.
